Our Legacy of Taste and Hospitality

AL Azeez Azad is more than just a hotel; it's a celebration of Indian heritage and culinary excellence. Nestled in the heart of the city, we've dedicated ourselves to perfecting the art of Indian cooking, passed down through generations. Our chefs, masters of traditional recipes, blend authentic spices with fresh, local ingredients to create dishes that tell a story.

From the moment you step in, you'll be enveloped in an ambiance that reflects the warmth and richness of India. We are committed to providing an unforgettable dining experience, ensuring every guest feels like royalty. Come, be a part of our story, and savor the magic.

Our Delicious Indian Delicacies

Indulge in a symphony of flavors with our meticulously crafted dishes. From aromatic biryanis to rich curries and tandoori delights, every bite is a journey to India.

Aromatic Chicken Biryani

A classic, fragrant basmati rice dish cooked with tender chicken, infused with saffron and a blend of secret spices.

Creamy Butter Chicken

Succulent pieces of chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce, lightly spiced and utterly delicious.

Spicy Paneer Tikka

Cubes of cottage cheese marinated in a spicy yogurt mixture, skewered and grilled to perfection in a tandoor.

Freshly Baked Naan

Soft, fluffy Indian bread, baked in our clay oven, perfect for scooping up our delicious curries.

Exotic Vegetable Korma

A rich and creamy stew of mixed vegetables, nuts, and exotic spices, truly a delight for the senses.

Crispy Vegetable Samosas

Deep-fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas, served with tangy chutneys – a perfect appetizer.
