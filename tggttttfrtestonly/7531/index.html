About Me

With a foundation deeply rooted in both aesthetics and functionality, I thrive on bringing ideas to life on the web. My journey began 7 years ago, driven by a fascination for how technology can solve real-world problems and enhance user interaction.

My expertise spans across modern web technologies, from robust back-end systems using Node.js and Python to captivating front-end interfaces with React and Vue. I believe in a holistic approach to web development, ensuring every project is not only visually stunning but also resilient, scalable, and a joy to use.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open source projects, or enjoying a good book. I'm always eager to learn and grow, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.
